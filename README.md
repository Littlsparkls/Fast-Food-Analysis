# 📊 Fast Food Consumption & Health Impact Analysis

## 🧾 Project Overview
This project explores the relationship between fast food consumption, lifestyle habits, and health outcomes such as Body Mass Index (BMI) and frequency of healthcare visits.

The goal is to uncover patterns and correlations that highlight how dietary behavior and physical activity influence overall health.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze the impact of fast food consumption on BMI  
- Examine the relationship between physical activity and health outcomes  
- Identify trends linking lifestyle habits to healthcare visits  
- Generate insights that support public health awareness and decision-making  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy)  
- **Data Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn  
- **Environment:** Jupyter Notebook  

---

## 🔍 Data Analysis Process

### 1. Data Cleaning
- Handled missing values  
- Ensured consistency in data types  
- Removed duplicates and irrelevant entries  

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Examined distributions of BMI, diet frequency, and activity levels  
- Identified trends and correlations between variables  

### 3. Data Visualization
- Created charts (bar plots, histograms, correlation heatmaps)  
- Visualized relationships between fast food intake and health indicators  

### 4. Insight Generation
- Interpreted patterns and relationships in the data  
- Derived meaningful conclusions from visualizations  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Higher fast food consumption is associated with increased BMI  
- Individuals with lower physical activity tend to have poorer health indicators  
- Frequent fast food intake may correlate with higher healthcare visits  
- Lifestyle choices play a significant role in long-term health outcomes  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: Kaggle  
- Features include:
  - Fast food consumption frequency  
  - Physical activity levels  
  - BMI  
  - Number of doctor visits
